# CarbonWise – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

### Product Name

CarbonWise

### Product Vision

CarbonWise helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple assessments, personalized insights, and actionable recommendations.

The goal is to make sustainability understandable and achievable for everyday people without requiring technical knowledge about carbon emissions.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Most people want to live more sustainably but face three major challenges:

1. They do not understand what a carbon footprint is.
2. They do not know which daily habits contribute most to their emissions.
3. They do not know which actions will have the biggest impact on reducing their footprint.

Existing solutions are often:

* Too technical
* Time-consuming
* Focused on raw numbers rather than practical actions

CarbonWise bridges this gap by translating lifestyle choices into understandable carbon impact and providing personalized guidance.

---

# 3. Target Audience

## Primary Audience

### Students

People who want to learn sustainable habits and understand their environmental impact.

### Young Professionals

Individuals interested in making environmentally responsible lifestyle choices.

### Sustainability Beginners

Users who have little or no knowledge about carbon footprints.

---

## User Characteristics

* Environmentally curious
* Not experts in climate science
* Mobile-first users
* Prefer simple and visual experiences
* Want actionable recommendations rather than complex reports

---

# 4. Product Goals

CarbonWise should help users:

### Understand

Learn what carbon footprints are and what contributes to them.

### Track

Measure their estimated monthly carbon footprint.

### Reduce

Receive personalized recommendations to lower emissions.

### Visualize Impact

See how lifestyle changes affect future emissions.

---

---

# 6. Core User Journey

## Step 1: Landing Page

User arrives on CarbonWise.

They learn:

* What CarbonWise is
* Why carbon footprints matter
* How the app can help

Primary CTA:
"Calculate My Footprint"

---

## Step 2: Assessment

User completes a short questionnaire.

Categories:

* Transportation
* Energy Usage
* Food Habits
* Waste Management

Estimated completion time:
2–3 minutes

---

## Step 3: Results Generation

CarbonWise calculates:

* Estimated monthly footprint
* Category breakdown
* CarbonWise Score

---

## Step 4: Dashboard

User views:

* Total emissions
* CarbonWise Score
* Visual breakdown of emission sources

---

## Step 5: Personalized Insights

User receives recommendations based on their highest-impact emission categories.

Each recommendation includes:

* Suggested action
* Reasoning
* Estimated impact

---

## Step 6: What-If Simulation

User selects possible lifestyle changes.

CarbonWise shows:

* Estimated emission reduction
* Updated CarbonWise Score
* Future footprint projection

---

## Step 7: Learn & Explore

User visits educational content.

Topics:

* Carbon footprint basics
* Sources of emissions
* Sustainability myths

---

# 7. Feature Prioritization

## Must-Have Features (MVP)

### 1. Carbon Footprint Assessment

Purpose:
Collect lifestyle information and estimate emissions.

Requirements:

* Multi-step questionnaire
* Progress indicator
* Input validation

---

### 2. Carbon Calculation Engine

Purpose:
Convert assessment responses into estimated emissions.

Requirements:

* Category-level calculations
* Total footprint calculation
* CarbonWise Score generation

---

### 3. Dashboard

Purpose:
Provide a visual summary of emissions.

Requirements:

* Total footprint display
* Category breakdown
* CarbonWise Score
* Charts and visualizations

---

### 4. Personalized Insights Engine

Purpose:
Recommend actions based on user behavior.

Requirements:

* Dynamic recommendations
* Impact estimates
* Explanations for each recommendation

---

### 5. What-If Simulator

Purpose:
Allow users to explore future improvements.

Requirements:

* Adjustable behavior changes
* Emission reduction estimates
* Updated score preview

---

### 6. Learn Hub

Purpose:
Increase awareness and education.

Requirements:

* Carbon footprint overview
* Sustainability basics
* Emission sources explained

---

### 7. Myth Busters

Purpose:
Correct common misconceptions.

Requirements:

* Myth vs Reality cards
* Easy-to-understand explanations

# 10. Non-Goals (Deliberately Not Building)

The following are intentionally out of scope:

❌ Carbon credit marketplace

❌ Carbon offset purchasing

❌ Social media platform

❌ Community forums

❌ Real-time utility integrations

❌ IoT device connections

❌ News feed

❌ Sustainability e-commerce

❌ Advanced climate analytics

❌ Enterprise sustainability tools

These features increase complexity without supporting the challenge objectives.

---

# 11. Design Principles

## Simplicity First

Users should complete the assessment in under 3 minutes.

## Action Over Information

Every screen should help users take action.

## Educational but Not Overwhelming

Teach users without creating information overload.

## Mobile-Friendly

Design for mobile responsiveness from the start.

## Accessibility

* Keyboard navigation
* High color contrast
* Responsive layouts
* Semantic UI components

---

# 12. Product Positioning

CarbonWise is not a carbon calculator.

CarbonWise is a personal sustainability companion that helps users:

Understand their environmental impact,
Track their carbon footprint,
And discover practical ways to reduce it.
